First feedback

My first feedback, sept 14, 2023

Motivate your study choice
Let hierbij op de zinsopbouw. Je verhaal vloeit goed en beschrijft je studiekeuze niet. Op het moment beschrijft het vooral je ervaring met ICT. Je hebt er wel staan dat je zelf een pc/toetsenbord hebt gemaakt. Voor je studiekeuze is dit interessant. Hier zou je meer op in kunnen gaan; waarom heb je voor ICT gekozen en niet technische informatica elders?

For the study choice blog I elaborated on this subject a lot more. Even though it's still hard for me to describe my choice, as I am generally a very indecisive person, I managed to elaborate and add some more information to my study choice. I also agree that the original motivation was a bit vague and incomplete, so I fully agree with this feedback.

After completing the study
Ik zou "Ik hoop over 4 jaar de weg te kennen" vervangen voor "ik hoop over 4 jaar een overzicht te hebben over mogelijke richtingen". Je weg kennen in heel ICT lukt niemand.

I have changed this in my SWOT blog, as I agree that is quite a big thing to want to achieve. I agree and changed it because I would mainly learn enough about ICT to get a sense of the direction I want to take in the field. There's indeed no need to know the whole ICT field..

Strengths
Ziet er netjes uit

I am glad to hear this as it's a topic I really struggle to find answers to. I've done my best to find, decide on and put the answers into words.

Weaknesses
Ik weet niet of je "andere gebruiken" neer wilt zetten. Misschien is het mogelijk om communiceren wat meer uit te werken

I am only now realizing I put this down, when I meant to put down that I can struggle with understanding others. In my conversation with my SLC I also had to elaborate some more on this topic, so I will also be adding the rest of that conversation into my blog.

opportunities
Dit zegt vrij weinig. Je kan de reden dat ICT een groot deel is van je leven wel nog extra beschrijven in je motivatie. Bij opportunities zou ik echter directer denken. Zijn er dingen die je uit de opleiding hoopt te halen? Denk aan voorbeelden zoals (maar niet gelimiteerd aan) 'leren presenteren', 'leren omgaan met perspectieven' of 'rust en overzicht kunnen delen met andere studenten'.

I also see now that I did not understand the question for "opportunities" correctly. I answered mostly to the question of what opportunities ICT can bring me in my life, and not what opportunities I will get at school. Some opportunities for me would be getting to know people with similar interests, and learning to plan, as I am very bad at planning and tend to not be motivated enough to actually write things down instead of just staring and overthinking the assignment, until the very last moment.

Threats
Programmeerervaring is niet echt een gevaar binnen je opleiding, daarvoor ga je namelijk naar school. Dit zou eerder een opportunity zijn. Dat je gaat verhuizen is wel een goede. Probeer hier wat meer concrete voorbeelden te plaatsen "tijdens projecten val ik snel weg vanwege mijn tekort aan programmeerervaring'. In mijn eigen document had ik "omdat ik met een negatieve score ben weggegaan op de HRO, kunnen toetsen en slechte resultaten snel zorgen voor paniek'.

Even though it seemed like the biggest threat to me when applying, I will have to revisit this and think for myself to what other threats I could encounter. I would say to begin with, my weaknesses are also big threats for me, as once I am too far behind and realize the deadlines are stacking up, I tend to avoid the problem instead of doing something about it. I also believe this is connected to one of my other threats. I tend to become afraid of failure; but instead of perfectionism I tend to not try at all. It's hard for me to put a lot of effort into something I know I might fail, since "If I didn't try I can't have failed it". I am scared of trying my hardest and eventually being confronted with the fact that, even though I tried my hardest, put so much time and effort in, I still failed.

Job and free time
Hobbies?

I am just now realizing I did not put any hobbies down, as I was too focused on the other parts and my job. I did put my hobbies on my profile page.
Most of the feedback was quite useful for me, as I ended up misunderstanding some of the questions that were asked. I got to revisit the questions, think about what my answers would be now/instead, and understand what views I had that were too vague and what things I need to be careful of.
