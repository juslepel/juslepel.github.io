How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

  • Select the printer for your job: HZ Printer
  • When the print job is given, log in using the HZ pass or by entering your HZ username and password
  • Then choose the print release option to see which print jobs are ready
  • If there is a sufficient balance in your print account, the print jobs will be printed
  • After printing, log out of the printer

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

  • Log on to the printer using your HZ card or by entering your HZ username amd password
  • Choose the Scan option in the menu
  • Place the original(s) on the feeder or glass plate
  • Press start
  • After scanning, log out from the printer

How can I buy something on the HZ webshop?

  • Go towebshop.hz.nl
  • Look through the website for the item you're looking for
  • click on the item and choose the quantity you wish for
  • click Add to shopping cart, the blue button on the bottom right of your screen
  • Once you have added your item, go to the menu on the top right of your screen
  • Click shopping cart and go to checkout
  • Follow the steps on your screen to pay
  • Now wait for your item to arrive!

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

  • Visit New location reservation
  • Check the Project group space type option to see all project group rooms that are available to reserve
  • Select location by clicking and dragging on the planner
  • Fill in your information and the additional info asked
  • Confirm reservation

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

  • Navigate to the car park at Poelendaelesingel 12
  • for disabled parking head to Het Groene woud 1-3
  • At the barrier of the car park, ring the bell or present your HZ pass
  • Park at the designated HZ parking spots, marked with a white sign with HZ logo
  • From here you can reach the school via the pedestrian tunnel
