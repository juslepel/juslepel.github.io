Personal SWOT analysis

My personal SWOT analysis, sept 14, 2023

What are your strong points?
I am good at and enjoy tasks and assignments with a specific goal. Besides that I think for myself a lot, and am good at staying calm in busy and stressful situations

Wat are your weakpoints?
I can struggle with communicating and understanding others in situations where they have a different thoughtprocess from me. This is something I am aware of cause I tend to approach some things in different ways than others. I try to be mindful of this and focus on the way other's have interpreted sentences, but sometimes I forget this and there ends up being a lot of confusion and miscommunication. It's difficult for me to change the way I approach and think about things, but when I stay aware of it, I have noticed things going a lot smoother.

Which opportunities do you see?
An opportunity for me at this school would be getting to know other people with similar interests. I also think it's a big opportunity for me to practice with planning, as I am very bad at planning and tend to not be motivated enough. I end up staring at and overthinking the subject, instead of actually starting it and going through the steps. This makes for a lot of panic and urgency in the last moment.

Which risks do you see?
I moved to a different part of the country, so I now have to adjust to living on my own, getting to know everything here, and staying in contact with my friends and family. I think another threat for me would be that I can struggle with deadlines. Once they are stacking up and I am behind, I tend to avoid the problem instead of doing something about it and taking action. This is connected to another threat of mine; I can become quite afraid of failure. Instead of perfectionism, this causes me to not try at all. It's hard for me to put a lot of effort into something if I might fail. If the risk is very big I tend to not do anything for it, since "If I didn't try, I can't have failed it". I am scared of trying my hardest and eventually being confronted with the fact that, even though I tried my hardest and put in so much tie and effort, I still failed.
